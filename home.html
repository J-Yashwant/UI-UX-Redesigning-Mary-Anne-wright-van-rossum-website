Mary-Anne Wright Van Ross

Mary-Anne is currently working from her studio in France, and in mid 2024 from her studio in Devon. Her last major solo exhibition was at a beautiful winery in Valflaunes, Aveyron in Southern France at Terres des Cambon in Pic St Loup, near Montpelier. The winery changed hands in 2022 and is now called Domain Uma. It is a superb venue and produces wonderful wine in the Pic St Loup region.

Mary-Anne's gallery / studio in Okehampton, Devon, England is now open and viewing is by appointment only.

To register an interest in Art Classes, contact Mary-Anne at 07776915738 or maryanne.arts1@gmail.com
